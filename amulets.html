Thai Amulets: Sacred Protection

A Thai Buddha amulet (Phra Kruang) is a type of Thai Buddhist blessed item. It is used for raising funds to help the temple producing the amulets. Worshippers can obtain an amulet or a Thai Buddhist monk blessing by donating money or offering oil to the temple.

After the donation, the monk will give them an amulet as a gift. With the change of time, amulets are no longer simply a "gift", but a tool to help enhance luck in different aspects of life. People use amulets to improve their marriage, wealth, health, love and people relationships.

One of the most famous types is the Phra Somdej, often called the "King of Amulets", created by Somdej Toh.
